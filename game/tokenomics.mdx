## Total Supply

The total supply of the **CHIP** token is **10,000,000 tokens**, distributed between community allocations and team reserves to ensure sustainable growth of the Abyss ecosystem.

## Distribution Breakdown

### Community Allocation: 7,000,000 (70%)

The majority of CHIP tokens are allocated to the community through gameplay and ecosystem initiatives.

#### Gameplay Rewards: 6,000,000 (60%)
- Earned by playing the game
- Formula: `CHIP_EARNED = floor(SCORE / 20)`
- Boost promotions during launch phases

| Score | CHIP Earned |
|------:|------------:|
| 100   | 5           |
| 250   | 12          |
| 500   | 25          |
| 1000  | 50          |

#### NFT Drops: 500,000 (5%)
- Distributed through relic NFT mints
- Special event rewards
- Community competitions

#### Liquidity: 500,000 (5%)
- DEX liquidity provision
- Ensures healthy trading markets

### Team Allocation: 3,000,000 (30%)

#### Team & Development: 1,500,000 (15%)
- Core development team compensation
- Long-term alignment with ecosystem success

#### Treasury: 1,500,000 (15%)
- DAO-controlled treasury for ecosystem development
- Partnership funding
- Marketing and growth initiatives

## Session Pricing

<Note>
All game sessions are competitive and contribute to the prize pool.
</Note>

### Entry Cost
- **Fixed price: $1 USD equivalent**
- Payable in: **ETH, STRK, USDC, LORDS, CHIP**, or other allowed ERC-20 tokens
- Price calculated at session creation

### Revenue Distribution

| Destination | Share |
|-------------|------:|
| Prize Pool  | 50%   |
| Treasury    | 30%   |
| Team        | 20%   |

## CHIP Earning Boosts

Players earn CHIP tokens based on their final session score. During launch, promotional boosts increase earnings:

| Phase | Duration | Multiplier |
|-------|----------|:----------:|
| Launch | Week 1 | 1x |
| Boost | Weeks 2-4 | 4x |
| Sustain | After Week 4 | 2x (until exhausted) |

<Tip>
Play during the Boost phase (Weeks 2-4) to maximize your CHIP earnings with 4x multiplier!
</Tip>

## Value Accrual Mechanisms

### Prize Pool
- 50% of all session fees flow to the prize pool
- Distributed to top 5 leaderboard players each season
- Multiple tokens accumulate (ETH, STRK, etc.)

### Treasury
The CHIP treasury enables:
- **Buybacks** - Token buyback programs
- **Development** - Funding new features
- **Partnerships** - Ecosystem collaborations
- **Liquidity** - DEX liquidity incentives

## Relic NFT Integration

Relic NFTs provide in-game advantages and are part of the CHIP economy:

- **Minting cost** - CHIP tokens burned on mint
- **Limited supply** - Creates scarcity and collectibility
- **Tradeable** - Full ERC721 marketplace support

### Genesis Relics (First Drop)
- 5 unique relic types
- Powerful in-game abilities
- One-time exclusive mint

## No Lockups

- All CHIP earned through gameplay is immediately liquid
- No vesting schedules for player earnings
- Team tokens follow standard vesting

## Economic Sustainability

The Abyss economy is designed for long-term sustainability:

1. **Play-to-earn** - CHIP minted through gameplay rewards skill
2. **Prize pool** - Competitive sessions fund real prizes
3. **Burning mechanisms** - Relic mints reduce supply
4. **Treasury growth** - 30% of sessions fund development
