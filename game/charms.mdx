## What are Soul Charms?

**Soul Charms ($CHARM)** are a new NFT collection in Abyss that give you a permanent advantage in your games. Unlike temporary items, Charms are owned by you and can be brought into any session.

Primary benefits:
- **Boost Luck**: Increases the chance of symbols forming patterns via VRF bias.
- **Passive Effects**: Special bonuses like extra spins, pattern multipliers, or conditional luck.
- **Collectibility**: 20 unique charms across 4 rarity tiers (Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary).

## How Luck Works

Luck is a special stat provided by Charms that biases the **VRF randomness** in your favor.

- **Normal Spin**: Each symbol is completely random (independent).
- **With Luck**: The grid generation is biased to "copy" neighbors, making patterns more likely to form.
- **Higher Luck = Better Patterns**: The more Luck you have, the higher the probability of connecting huge horizontal, vertical, or diagonal lines.

## How to Obtain

1. **Minting**: At the end of every competitive game, you have a chance to mint a random Charm based on your score.
   - Chance = `min(Score / 125, 50)%`
2. **Shop**: Charms you own can appear in the in-game Market, allowing you to "equip" their effects for that session by buying them with points.
